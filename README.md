# Rowland Restorations

Welcome to the official website for **Rowland Restorations**, where I document and share all the projects, tools, and resources I’ve built over time — from classic muscle car rebuilds to embedded electronics and ECU tuning.

## 🔧 Home
The homepage gives a quick intro and showcases one of my featured projects — like the Camaro rebuild or Jet Ski ECU setup — to give new visitors a glimpse into what I do.

## 🚗 Projects
This section covers hands-on restoration and customization work, including:
- 1969 Camaro with modern suspension and LS swap
- 1973 Dodge Challenger frame and floor repair
- Kawasaki Jet Ski ECU reverse engineering and wiring
- Speeduino-based ignition control and Arduino tools

## 📚 How-To Guides
Step-by-step tutorials for electrical work, CAN sniffing, ECU diagnostics, and more. These are based on real builds and include wiring references, parts lists, and walkthroughs.

## 📥 Downloads
Useful files to support your own builds, including:
- XDF tuning files
- Wiring diagrams
- ECU bin files
- Source code
- PDF guides and notes
